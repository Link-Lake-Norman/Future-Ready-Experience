Week 5: Identity, Confidence & Growth Mindset

Your future is shaped by how you see yourself, how you respond to challenges, and your willingness to continue learning and growing.

Essential Question

How can I believe in my ability to grow and create my future?


Learning Objectives

  • Understand the difference between a fixed mindset and a growth mindset.
  • Recognize personal abilities that can continue developing.
  • Build confidence through reflection and action.
  • Practice responding to challenges with resilience.
