Week 2: Understanding Myself

The first step in building your future is understanding yourself — your experiences, your strengths, your interests, and what makes you unique.

Essential Question

What makes me uniquely me?


Learning Objectives

  • Identify personal characteristics that shape who you are.
  • Recognize experiences that have influenced your growth.
  • Understand how self-awareness impacts future decisions.
  • Begin developing confidence in your own story.
