Week 4: My Story Matters

Every person has a story. Your experiences, challenges, accomplishments, and lessons learned shape the person you are becoming.

Essential Question

How do my experiences shape who I am and who I can become?


Learning Objectives

  • Recognize the value of personal experiences.
  • Identify moments that shaped personal growth.
  • Practice communicating personal stories with confidence.
  • Understand how stories connect to future opportunities.
