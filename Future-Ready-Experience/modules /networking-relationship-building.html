Week 18: Networking & Relationship Building

Opportunities often begin with relationships. Learning how to connect with others is a skill that supports lifelong success.

Essential Question

How can I build relationships that support my future?


Learning Objectives

  • Understand the importance of networking.
  • Practice introducing yourself professionally.
  • Learn how relationships create opportunities.
  • Build confidence connecting with others.
