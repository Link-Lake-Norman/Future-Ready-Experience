Week 3: My Strengths, Interests & Values

Your future should connect with who you are. Understanding your strengths, interests, and values helps you identify opportunities where you can thrive.

Essential Question

What strengths, interests, and values make me unique?


Learning Objectives

  • Identify personal strengths and natural abilities.
  • Explore interests that create curiosity and motivation.
  • Understand personal values and why they matter.
  • Connect self-awareness to future opportunities.
