Week 17: AI, Technology & Human Skills

Technology is changing how we work. Future Ready™ students learn how to use technology while strengthening the human skills that make them valuable.

Essential Question

Learning Objectives

  • Understand how AI and technology impact careers.
  • Identify skills technology cannot replace.
  • Practice responsible technology use.
  • Prepare for future workplace changes.
