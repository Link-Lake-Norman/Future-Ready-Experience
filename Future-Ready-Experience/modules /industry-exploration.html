Week 15: Industry Exploration

Careers exist within industries that solve problems, create products, and serve communities. Understanding industries helps you discover opportunities.

Essential Question

Learning Objectives

  • Understand different industry sectors.
  • Explore how organizations create value.
  • Identify careers connected to industries.
  • Recognize local and global opportunities.
